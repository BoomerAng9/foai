# Content Policy

**Effective Date:** April 2026
**Applies to:** All users of the Deploy Platform, Per|Form, and all A.I.M.S.-powered applications

---

## Our Philosophy

We believe in your right to create. This platform exists to empower creators, entrepreneurs, analysts, athletes, and builders to produce real work with AI tools. We support free expression. We support controversial opinions. We support edgy content, adult language, political commentary, satire, parody, and everything else that makes creative work worth doing.

**We do not moderate opinion. We do not moderate taste. We do not moderate tone.**

We moderate one thing: **deplorable content** — content so vile that it constitutes genuine harm to real people. That's the line. Nothing else.

---

## What We Allow

Everything that doesn't cross the deplorable content line below. Including but not limited to:

- Edgy humor, dark comedy, satire, parody
- Adult language and profanity
- Controversial political, religious, or social commentary
- Criticism of public figures, corporations, governments, institutions
- Competitive or aggressive business content
- Content others might find offensive, disagreeable, or distasteful
- AI-generated voices, images, and video using transformative reference material
- Commercial content, advertising, marketing materials
- Content that receives external copyright claims (that's between you and the claimant — see Fair Use Disclaimer)

**Your content. Your voice. Your responsibility.**

---

## What We Do NOT Allow (Deplorable Content)

The following categories represent the only content that triggers enforcement action. This is a HIGH bar by design. We are not your parents, your HR department, or your social media moderator. But we will not host filth.

### Immediate and Permanent Ban (Zero Tolerance)

- **Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)** in any form — real, generated, implied, or depicted. Zero tolerance. Instant permanent ban. Reported to NCMEC and law enforcement. No warning. No appeal.
- **Content that sexually exploits minors** in any capacity — grooming, suggestive imagery, age-inappropriate sexualization

### One Warning, Then Permanent Ban

- **Targeted harassment campaigns** — repeated, directed abuse of a specific individual (not public criticism of public figures — actual targeted campaigns: doxxing, coordinated pile-ons, sustained personal attacks)
- **Doxxing** — publishing private personal information (home address, phone number, SSN, family members) of non-public individuals with malicious intent
- **Deepfake revenge pornography** — non-consensual intimate imagery of real, identifiable people, whether AI-generated or manipulated
- **Explicit incitement of real-world violence** against named individuals or identified groups — not "I disagree with X" but "go hurt X at this address"
- **Biological, chemical, or nuclear weapon construction instructions** — detailed, actionable instructions intended to enable harm
- **Graphic violence glorification targeting real named individuals** — not fictional violence, not video game content, not war reporting — content that celebrates or encourages real violence against a real named person

## Enforcement Process

### Detection

Content is monitored through two channels:
1. **Automated detection** via the Grammar content filter layer (internal, invisible to users)
2. **User reports** via the Report button available on all content surfaces

Automated flags are NEVER auto-bans. Every flag is reviewed by a human before any action is taken.

### First Offense — Warning

If you produce content that crosses the deplorable line (categories above, excluding CSAM which is zero-tolerance):

1. The specific content is removed immediately
2. You receive an email clearly stating:
   - Exactly what content was flagged
   - Which policy it violated
   - That this is your **one and only warning**
   - That a second offense results in permanent, irreversible account termination

The tone of this email is direct. Not corporate. Not mealy. You will understand exactly what you did and exactly what happens next if you do it again.

### Second Offense — Excommunicado

If you produce deplorable content a second time after receiving your warning:

1. Your account is **permanently terminated** effective immediately
2. All your content is removed from the platform
3. You receive a final email stating:
   - The specific violation
   - That your account is permanently terminated
   - That there is **no appeal process**
   - That you are not welcome back
4. Your email and identifying information are added to our permanent exclusion list
5. Attempting to create a new account results in immediate re-termination

**There is no negotiation. There is no "but I didn't mean it." There is no second chance after the second chance.**

We gave you a warning. You chose to do it again. You're done here.

---

## What We Do NOT Moderate

To be absolutely clear — the following will NEVER trigger enforcement, no matter how many people complain:

- Political speech you disagree with
- Religious views you find offensive
- Humor you don't find funny
- Opinions that are factually wrong
- Content that competes with your business
- AI-generated content that "sounds like" a public figure (transformative fair use)
- Content that received a DMCA claim on another platform
- Content that uses profanity, adult themes, or graphic (non-deplorable) imagery
- Content that is "disrespectful" — disrespect is not deplorable

**We are not the taste police.** If it's not deplorable, it stays.

---

## Reporting Content

If you encounter content you believe crosses the deplorable content line:

1. Click the **Report** button on the content
2. Select the category that applies
3. Provide a brief description of why you believe it's deplorable (not just disagreeable)
4. Our moderation team will review within 48 hours

Reports submitted because you simply disagree with the content will not result in action. We review for deplorable content, not hurt feelings.

---

## Appeals (First Offense Only)

If you received a first-offense warning and believe it was issued in error:

1. Reply to the warning email within 14 days
2. Explain specifically why you believe the content does not meet the deplorable threshold
3. Our moderation team will review and respond within 7 business days
4. If the warning is reversed, it is fully removed from your record

**There is no appeal for second-offense terminations.** Excommunicado is final.
