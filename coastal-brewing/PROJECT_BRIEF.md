# PROJECT_BRIEF — Coastal Brewing

## What this company is

Coastal Brewing is a **100% virtual coffee and tea company**, AI-managed, with a single human owner. The owner approves; ACHEEVY routes; Boomer_Angs perform; Chicken_Hawk reviews; AuditLedger records; OpenClaw executes within bounded permissions.

## What we sell at launch

- Coffee: house blend, dark roast, decaf
- Tea: breakfast, herbal, green
- Matcha: starter pack
- Bundles: discovery sampler
- Subscriptions: coffee, tea, coffee + tea (monthly)

## Fulfillment posture

**Supplier-fulfilled. Stepper-orchestrated. Owner-approved.** Default supplier: Temecula Coffee Roasters. We do not roast, warehouse, pick, or pack. The supplier ships under our brand once the wholesale account is opened, due-diligence is green, and the owner approves the relationship.

## Storefront strategy

Hold for **Hostinger Ecommerce** as the planned storefront. Until it is available, customer order intake runs through Taskade-hosted Stepper forms that webhook into the runner. Manual supplier fulfillment with owner approval on every order.

**No Shopify. No third-party storefront.** The runner + Stepper + future Hostinger Ecommerce is the entire commerce path.

## What we are not doing at launch

- Not buying a roaster
- Not opening a storefront
- Not hiring human employees for daily ops
- Not making organic / fair-trade / health claims without a Feynman verification receipt and owner approval
- Not running any automation that sends external messages or moves money without owner approval
- Not building on Shopify

## Path to roasting later

Demand-validation gate first. Coastal Brewing only considers owned roasting after:

1. Twelve consecutive months of subscription growth
2. A proven repeat-purchase rate that supports the unit economics of in-house roasting
3. An owner-approved capital plan
4. A founding-roaster hire signed and onboarded

Until those gates are met, we do not roast.

## Operating posture

Every action passes through the one-direction flow (`docs/00_one_direction_decision.md`). ACHEEVY is the sole router. Boomer_Angs handle department work. Chicken_Hawk reviews risk. AuditLedger records. OpenClaw executes within action policy. The owner approves the rest.

## Brand voice

Direct, coastal, calm. No over-promising. No invented provenance. No certification language until the cert is in hand and verified through a Feynman receipt.

## Success criteria for the first 90 days

- Zero unverified public claims
- Zero owner-bypass events
- A working subscription program with at least one cohort retained past month two
- A verified supplier relationship with a green due-diligence receipt
- A weekly operating receipt the owner reads and approves
- Hostinger Ecommerce migration plan agreed before storefront cutover
