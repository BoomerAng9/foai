# Route Rules

## Route to NVIDIA

Use NVIDIA for work that is low-risk, repetitive, and easy to review:

- social captions
- draft product descriptions
- email subject-line variants
- support classification
- review clustering
- daily summaries
- outreach draft variants
- blog outlines
- FAQ drafts

## Route to Feynman

Use Feynman when the task needs source-backed evidence:

- supplier due diligence
- organic/fair-trade verification
- certification proof
- market intelligence
- competitor research
- product claim review
- origin notes
- public-copy evidence checks

## Route to Premium/Owner

Use premium model review plus owner approval when the task has risk:

- final public claims
- health/FDA wording
- organic/fair-trade labeling
- legal filings
- contracts
- payment setup
- refunds above threshold
- supplier changes
- ad budget changes
- anything irreversible

## Route to OpenClaw

OpenClaw executes only after route outcome and approval state are clear.
