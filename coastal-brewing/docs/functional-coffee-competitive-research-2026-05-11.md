## Price-Per-Ounce Benchmark

### Functional / Mushroom Coffee (instant or blended powder)
- **Low band:** Rasa $3.37/oz, RYZE $5.66/oz (powder is dense — per-serving math matters more than oz)
- **Median:** Most instant functional coffee sells at **$1.00–$1.50 per serving** on subscription
- **High band:** Clevr $1.70/serving, Everyday Dose $1.60/serving

### Real Specialty Coffee (12oz whole bean / ground)
- **Atlas:** ~$1.60/oz
- **Trade Premium:** ~$2.00/oz
- **Blue Bottle Single-Origin:** ~$2.00–2.50/oz
- **Peak State (functional + real beans):** ~$1.85–2.10/oz

### Functional-vs-Regular Premium
- Functional / mushroom coffee carries a **15–35% premium** over equivalent non-functional specialty coffee on a per-serving basis
- The premium is bigger in **instant powders** (RYZE, MUD\WTR) because cost of goods is lower and margin is pricing the "functional" claim
- The premium is **smaller in real-bean functional** (Peak State) because the bean itself already commands a specialty price

## Founder-Brand Positioning Notes

- **Everyday Dose** anchors with "$1.50/cup" — never quotes the full bag price up front
- **RYZE** anchors with "$1.00/cup" — sub-dollar is the psychological hook
- **MUD\WTR** anchors with the **annual prepaid** ($100 for 90 servings) — pushes the per-serving math down to $1.11
- **Magic Mind** uses **trial-pricing** ($19.99 first 15 days, then $59.25/mo) — masks the recurring rate
- **Peak State** positions on **"real coffee with mushrooms"** — explicitly calls out instant-blend competitors as lower-grade
- **Four Sigmatic** uses BOGO + 65% off first order to mask the $50 list — recurring is $35

### Recurring observation
**Nobody quotes a single-bag monthly price north of ~$50 without a multi-bag bundle, a starter kit, or a premium-tier positioning that explains why.** $40 is the soft ceiling for a single 30-serving functional coffee pouch on monthly sub.

## Recommended Coastal Pricing

| Coastal SKU | Current | Recommended | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Coffee Monthly** (regular specialty, 12oz) | $39.49 | **$24–28** | Match Trade Premium ($21.99) / Atlas (~$19) — Coastal's premium-roast brand can sit at top of band, not above |
| **Functional Coffee Monthly** (Lion's Mane / Cordyceps / Reishi, 12oz whole bean) | n/a (not listed) | **$32–36** | Sits between Peak State (~$25) and Four Sigmatic ($35), justified by real-bean + provenance + Sal/LUC haggle layer |
| **Tea Monthly** (3oz tin loose leaf) | $30.99 | **$16–20** | Harney & Sons 3oz tin is **$15 retail** — $30.99 is 2× retail with no clear justification; $18 on sub matches Harney sub-tier |
| **Combo Monthly** (1 coffee + 1 tea) | $67.49 | **$42–48** | Bundle of recommended Coffee Monthly ($26) + Tea Monthly ($18) at small bundle premium |

### Upper-band owners
- **Magic Mind** ($59.25 / mo) — but it's a **shot**, not a coffee, and explicitly premium-nootropic positioning
- **MUD\WTR** ($40 / 30-serving tin) — owns the upper band for instant functional with broad brand awareness

### Lower-band owners
- **RYZE** ($30 / 30-serving) — owns the entry tier with sub-dollar-per-cup hook
- **Atlas Coffee Club** ($19 / 12oz including ship) — owns specialty discovery tier

### Coastal's strategic position
Coastal should NOT compete on price — but it also cannot price 2-3× the band without a visible reason (provenance, real beans, founder narrative, Sal/LUC haggle UX). The recommended bands above put Coastal at the **top of normal market range** while preserving the negotiation envelope to land Custeez in the **$20–24 / mo** sweet spot via Sal/LUC discount authority.
