## 2. Per-Bag 12oz Specialty Range (this geography)

- **Floor:** $16.75 (Lowcountry organic blend)
- **Mid (most common blends):** **$17–$22**
- **Single origin range:** **$22–$30**
- **Premium / micro-lot ceiling:** **$33–$46** (PERC limited drops)
- **Outlier ultra-rare:** $167 / 1kg competition lot (Second State)

**Working median for "everyday 12oz blend" in Charleston/Savannah specialty: ≈ $18–$20.**
**Working median for single-origin 12oz: ≈ $22–$26.**

This validates the existing Coastal anchor canon (Tier A 12oz $24.99 / Tier F $32.99) sitting **at or just above local single-origin median** — a defensible premium **only if pillars + experience justify it**.

## 3. Annual Membership — Is It a Thing Locally?

**Effectively no.** Across 10 roasters scraped, **zero offer an annual coffee-club membership** with welcome box / auto-discount / free delivery built in. The patterns observed are:

- **Recurring subscription (cancel-anytime)** — PERC, CCR, Counter Culture, Lowcountry, Three Tree, Second State. Cadence 1–6 weeks. Discount range 5–15%. Free shipping at $30–$40 thresholds.
- **Gift subscription** (3-, 6-, 12-month prepaid) — PERC + Counter Culture only. Closest analog to "annual" but **gift-positioned**, not member-positioned.
- **Build-A-Box / Sampler** — CCE, Second State. One-time or recurring.
- **Tiered loyalty / annual fee** — **none found**.

**Translation:** an explicit annual-membership product (welcome kit + free delivery + auto-discount + concierge layer) is a **white-space play** in Coastal Carolina + Coastal Georgia. No competitor currently occupies that slot.

## 4. Local-Market Premium Positioning — What Would a Pooler/Savannah Custee Pay?

**Comparable annual spend already happening (un-bundled):**
- 1 bag/wk @ $20 avg × 52 = **$1,040/yr unmanaged + per-order shipping**
- 1 bag/2wk @ $22 single-origin × 26 = **$572/yr**
- Casual 1 bag/month × 12 = **$240–$360/yr**

**What a SE-coast Custee will accept for an annual membership tier (estimated):**
- **Floor membership:** **$249–$299/yr** — roughly = the ~$240 they'd already spend monthly + perceived "free shipping value" + welcome box. Easy yes for current monthly buyers.
- **Standard membership:** **$499–$599/yr** — bi-weekly bag + free local delivery + welcome box + member pricing on accessories. This matches **what they're already spending unmanaged**, repackaged as a membership with extras.
- **Curation / Concierge tier:** **$999–$1,499/yr** — weekly bag + Sal/LUC/ACHEEVY agentic curation + monthly tasting flight + visit-Custee local benefit (Pooler radius). **No local competitor offers anything like this.**
- **Wholesale Direct (B2B office / café):** **$2,500–$5,000/yr** annual commit, mirrors PERC wholesale tier (no public pricing — gated). Coastal can lead on transparency.

**Pillar layering on top** (Curation / Experience / Provenance) is consistent with the existing 3-pillar matrix — none of the local competitors have a published tiered-pillar pricing structure.
