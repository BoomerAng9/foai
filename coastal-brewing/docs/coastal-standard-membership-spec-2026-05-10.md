## 2. Pricing math

| Line item | Value to Custee | Cost to Coastal | Margin contribution |
|---|---|---|---|
| Annual fee | $199 | — | $199 (recognized over 12 months) |
| Welcome box (ceramic dripper + stickers + 50g Habbak tin) | ~$45 retail | ~$22 landed | -$22 (CAC absorbed, one-time) |
| Ongoing 15% member discount on order volume | varies | -15% margin per order | absorbed; offset by retention uplift |
| Free delivery (under $15 freight) | varies | -$8.50 avg per shipment | absorbed; ~$50/yr per active member |
| Referral refund (max once, requires 2 new paid members) | -$199 if triggered | — | net-zero on referrer; +$398 in new gross fees |

**Net first-year contribution per non-referrer member (avg 6 orders/year):**
$199 (fee) − $22 (welcome box CAC) − $50 (delivery absorption) − $90 (15% margin sacrifice on $600 GMV) = **+$37 contribution**

**Net first-year contribution per referrer member (with 2 successful referrals):**
$199 (referrer's fee, refunded) + 2 × $199 (new referred fees) − 3 × $22 welcome box CAC − 3 × $50 delivery absorption = **+$166 net + 2 new long-tenure members**

The referral mechanic is positive-sum because the referred members carry their own fees forward. Blackbird relies on the same math.

## 4. Terms and conditions (full)

```
Coastal Brewing Co. Standard Membership — Terms

1. MEMBERSHIP FEE
   $199 per year, charged on signup and each subsequent annual
   renewal. Paid via our secure payment processor at checkout.

2. RENEWAL
   Memberships auto-renew on the anniversary date unless cancelled
   at least 7 calendar days before the renewal date. Cancellation is
   self-service via your Coastal Brewing Co. account or by emailing
   members@coastalbrewing.co.

3. FREE DELIVERY
   Members get free standard delivery on any order whose carrier
   freight cost is under $15. Heavy, oversized, or freight-class
   shipments (e.g. case orders, drum-roaster accessories, ceramic
   gear sets) may carry residual freight calculated at checkout. Free
   delivery applies to physical-product shipments only. Coffee
   subscription deliveries are always free regardless of freight
   class as part of the subscription benefit.

4. 15% MEMBER DISCOUNT
   Automatic 15% off all retail-priced products at checkout. Stacks
   with the membership free-delivery benefit. Does NOT stack with
   bulk-pricing tiers (B2B pricing is already at-cost+).

5. WELCOME BOX
   Shipped within 10 business days of signup to the address on file.
   Contains a ceramic pour-over dripper, a Coastal Brewing Co.
   storefront-window-etching sticker set, and a complimentary 50g
   tin of Habbak (Saudi Hassawi mint, sourced from our Lorton,
   Virginia tea partner). Welcome box value is part of the
   membership fee and is non-refundable once shipped.

6. REFERRAL REFUND
   Refer two (2) new paid members within twelve (12) months of your
   own signup, and your initial $199 fee is refunded to your
   original payment method within 30 days of the second referral
   completing payment. "New paid member" means a person who:
     (a) has never been a Coastal Brewing Co. retail customer,
         subscriber, or member before;
     (b) signs up for a Standard Membership using your unique
         referral code;
     (c) completes their first annual fee payment without refund.
   Renewing members and existing Custees do not qualify as referrals.
   Referral refund is one-time per member account — the second-year
   renewal fee is not eligible for further refund.

7. CANCELLATION + REFUNDS
   Members may cancel within 7 days of initial signup for a full
   refund, provided the welcome box has not been shipped. After 7
   days OR after welcome-box shipment (whichever comes first),
   memberships are non-refundable except via the referral mechanism
   in §6.

8. SUSPENSION
   Coastal Brewing Co. reserves the right to suspend memberships
   that engage in referral-system abuse (synthetic accounts, family
   networks treated as "new paid members," chargeback patterns).
   Suspension does not entitle the member to a refund.

9. CHANGES
   These terms may be updated with 30 days' notice via email and
   posting on coastalbrewing.co/membership. Changes take effect
   for the next renewal cycle.

10. CONTACT
    Questions: members@coastalbrewing.co
    Pooler, GA  ·  Made in PLR  ·  Coastal Brewing Co.
```

## 6. What this PRD is NOT

- **Not a lifetime offering.** Lifetime tiers were retired from Coastal canon 2026-05-11 — lifetime is now the AIMS / Plug-Me-In licensee tier (technology stack). Coastal beverages are annual/subscription only. See `lifetime-tier-positioning-2026-05-11.md`.
- **Not a B2B offering.** Wholesale + office coffee programs (per Dimension 2 of the billing matrix) have their own at-cost pricing and don't need member discounts.
- **Not a points/loyalty program.** This is flat-fee membership with referral mechanics — not punch-card or cumulative-spend rewards.

## 8. Worked example (Custee onboarding)

```
USER: signs up for Standard Membership at coastalbrewing.co/membership
  → Stripe Checkout charges $199, mints subscription
  → /api/membership/signup creates DB record:
      { user_id, signed_up_at, referral_code: "SAL-A8K2LN", welcome_box_status: 'queued' }
  → Stripe webhook fires customer.subscription.created
  → Welcome-box ship task lands in ops queue (Sal_Ang sees it on next check)
  → Confirmation email goes out: "Welcome to Coastal Brewing Co. — your V60, stickers,
     and Habbak ship within 10 business days. Your referral code is SAL-A8K2LN."

USER: places first coffee order
  → Stripe coupon MEMBER_15 auto-applied at 15%
  → Free shipping flag set (freight under $15 ceiling)
  → Order ships next-day from Pooler, GA

USER: refers two coworkers via referral code
  → Each coworker pays $199 + completes first month
  → /api/membership/referrals?code=SAL-A8K2LN returns count=2
  → Refund job fires automatically (or with one-button approval from Melli):
      Stripe refund $199 → original payment method
      Email goes out: "Your referral refund is on the way — you saved $199.
        Both your members are coffee-funded for the year. Coastal Brewing Co. salutes."
```
