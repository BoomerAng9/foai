# AIMS-SDK LTD market research — 2025-2026 trends (second pass)

**Date:** 2026-05-11
**Status:** Research brief — feeds final pricing bands for `aims-sdk-licensee-ratified-2026-05-11.md`
**Scope:** AppSumo + Dealify + PitchGround + SaaS Mantra + StackSocial state 2025-2026; the one-year-plus-lifetime two-tier pattern; "lifetime with disclaimer" mechanic; revised AIMS-SDK pricing proposal.

## 1. AppSumo 2025-2026

- **Revenue down ~50% over two years.** CEO Noah Kagan disclosed the drop on X; community estimate **~40% of AppSumo LTDs fail within 3 years.** [ppc.land](https://ppc.land/appsumos-revenue-crashes-50-as-lifetime-deal-model-faces-existential-crisis/)
- **Commission flip toward sellers.** New-buyer revenue share now **95% to seller (5% processing)**; returning-buyer share **70% seller / 30% AppSumo.** Negotiated per-deal. Reversal of the old 30/70 split that drove vendor walk-offs. [AppSumo seller docs](https://sell.appsumo.com/) · [Help Center](https://help.appsumo.com/article/112-seller-revenue-share)
- **AI-era LTD model shift.** AppSumo's own playbook now mandates **capped credits + scheduled refresh + BYOK** as default for AI deals — explicitly to make the math work. "Unlimited AI" deals are flagged as unsustainable. [AppSumo Blog · Lifetime deals in AI era](https://appsumo.com/blog/lifetime-deals-in-ai-era)
- **Disclaimer normalization.** "We Got Your Back" guarantee = 100% credit (Plus) / 50% credit (non-Plus) **only if a Select tool shuts down within 12 months.** After 12 months, no refund. Customers are visibly tired but adoption hasn't collapsed. [AppSumo Help Center](https://help.appsumo.com/article/698-wegotyourbackguarantee)
- **Average deal price band 2025-2026:** $49-$199 single license, sweet spot **$59-$89**. T3 stacks land $399-$499. [99signals](https://www.99signals.com/appsumo-deals/) · [PCSaaS](https://pcsaas.com/appsumo-vs-stacksocial-comparison/)
- **Notable shutdowns + backlash:** iBrave web hosting (sold "lifetime" via StackSocial, shut Nov 1 2024 — still selling 20 days prior); Fable (refund denied per X user Jan). Indie Hackers + Trustpilot threads document the trust erosion. [DEV Community](https://dev.to/productimpossible/lifetime-subscriptions-dont-mean-what-you-think-they-mean-1hg7) · [Indie Hackers](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/if-you-have-lifetime-deals-ltd-but-the-saas-shuts-down-where-does-that-leave-you-1e7bf5ef82)

## 2. AppSumo positioning vs Dealify

- **Dealify is an aggregator, not a marketplace** — clicks bounce to the seller's site (AppSumo, PitchGround, vendor direct). Lower commission posture; shares campaign insight with sellers. [Dealify · alternatives](https://dealify.com/blogs/our-blog-popular-posts/appsumo-alternatives) · [Qrolic comparison](https://qrolic.com/blog/appsumo-vs-dealify-comparison/)
- **Volume gap remains large** — AppSumo's catalog + traffic dwarfs Dealify's; Dealify wins on seller economics, AppSumo wins on velocity.
- **AppSumo Originals** are first-party products sold by AppSumo (marketing/productivity); no significant ecosystem impact. [HasThemes guide](https://hasthemes.com/blog/how-to-sell-your-product-on-appsumo-marketplace/)

## 3. PitchGround / SaaS Mantra / StackSocial

- **PitchGround** — 4.7/5 Trustpilot (393 reviews) but G2 reports non-payment complaints + "feels dead." Founded 2018, 90%-off positioning, smaller deal-flow than AppSumo. [G2](https://www.g2.com/products/pitchground/reviews)
- **SaaS Mantra** — beta-product co-development model (buyers help fund development). Heavier vendor collaboration, smaller catalog. [SaaSPirate alternatives](https://saaspirate.com/appsumo-alternatives/)
- **StackSocial** — broadest catalog (software + courses + physical gadgets + lifestyle). Weakest refund policy (30-day store credit only if unclaimed). Carried the iBrave shutdown. [PCSaaS](https://pcsaas.com/appsumo-vs-stacksocial-comparison/)

## 4. Two-tier (1-year + lifetime) pattern is common in 2025-2026

The owner instinct is correct — pairing a higher-priced annual with a lower-priced lifetime is standard pattern now, not unusual. Examples:

- **AgenticFlow** (AppSumo) — annual + LTD, BYOK across OpenAI/Anthropic/Google/Straico. [AppSumo · AgenticFlow](https://appsumo.com/products/agenticflow/)
- **Diaflow** — LTD at **$89**, separate metered/annual plan; BYOK for OpenAI/Anthropic/DeepSeek. [Findfahim review](https://findfahim.com/productivity-software/diaflow-reviews-lifetime-deal/)
- **TypeThink AI** — LTD BYOK Pro at **$59**, separate subscription path; supports OpenAI/Claude/Gemini. [TypeThink](https://typethink.ai/lifetime-deal)
- **Triplo AI** — BYOK on all tiers; LTD + monthly paths coexist. [Findfahim review](https://findfahim.com/productivity-software/triplo-ai-review/)
- **TubeOnAI** — LTD with BYOK requiring OpenAI key; annual path separate.
- **Apper** — BYOK + LTD on AppSumo with separate credit-bundle path. [Lifetimo](https://lifetimo.com/deal/apper-lifetime-deal/)

**Spread pattern:** lifetime entry ~$59-$89; annual equivalent runs $300-$1,200/yr depending on tier. Lifetime acts as acquisition; annual acts as the support-included path for serious buyers.

## 5. "Lifetime with disclaimer" — normalized

- **Standard SaaS practice:** "lifetime" means **operational life of the company**, not the customer's. Hosting providers cap at 10-15 years. Most LTDs now include "subject to change" + "we reserve the right to modify" language. [Vedanta Software analysis](https://vedantasoftware.com/blog/13618/the-hidden-truth-of-lifetime-subscriptions---memberships-in-saas-and-software-products) · [DEV Community](https://dev.to/productimpossible/lifetime-subscriptions-dont-mean-what-you-think-they-mean-1hg7)
- **Customer reaction:** mixed — some backlash on shutdown events, but no organized boycott of LTDs as a category. Buyer expectations have adjusted; the disclaimer is now table-stakes, not a red flag.
- **AppSumo's own AI-era guidance** explicitly endorses BYOK + capped credits + clear top-up pricing as the sustainable LTD pattern.

## 6. Implications for AIMS-SDK

The 2025-2026 market validates every owner ratification:

1. **Two-tier (annual + lifetime)** is the dominant pattern, not the exception.
2. **BYOK** is the explicit sustainability mechanic AppSumo itself endorses.
3. **"Subject to change" disclaimer** is normalized — pricing the lifetime tier lower is supported by market behavior.
4. **Whitelabel without source code** matches DM Champ / SiteGPT / Chatbase pattern. [LifetimeDealTech 2026 list](https://lifetimedealtech.com/white-label-ai-agents-for-agencies-2026/)
5. **Template-not-platform** matches every successful 2025-2026 LTD — buyers expect to customize, not inherit your business.

## Proposed revised pricing — two-deal × three-tier

All prices in USD. BYOK Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite + AIMS GUI in Deploy Platform on every tier. Template only — no Coastal data, no live AIMS matrix.

**BOTH deals are pay-once.** No recurring billing on either. The "1-year deal" buys 12 months of managed-service support; the "lifetime deal" buys ongoing access with the disclaimer. Same engine, different service envelope.

| Tier | Lifetime deal (with disclaimer) | 1-year managed deal (full SLA) | Scope |
|---|---|---|---|
| **T1 Starter** | **$89** one-time | **$348** one-time (12 months managed) | 1 vertical, 1 admin, hosted, no resale |
| **T2 Growth** *(stackable)* | **$199** one-time / code, stack to 3 codes | **$948** one-time (12 months managed) | 3 verticals → 9 stacked, 5 sub-accounts → 15 stacked, hosted, resale |
| **T3 Enterprise** *(whitelabel)* | **$499** one-time | **$2,388** one-time (12 months managed) | Unlimited verticals + sub-accounts, own-domain whitelabel, resale, no source code |

**Rationale:**
- T1 LTD at **$89** sits at the AppSumo AI-tool sweet spot (Diaflow $89, Triplo/TypeThink $59-$89). 1-year managed at $348 = ~4× LTD, in line with the AgenticFlow/Diaflow spread for the managed-service upgrade.
- T2 stacking unlocks linear scale (3 codes = T1-T2-T3 of the prior 3-tier proposal). LTD-to-managed multiplier ~4.8×.
- T3 whitelabel **$499** matches enterprise-LTD ceilings on AppSumo (typical T3 stack ~$399-$499). 1-year managed at $2,388 = ~4.8× LTD.
- Lifetime entry kept low because of the disclaimer (subject-to-change). 1-year managed priced for explicit-SLA value + hands-on onboarding included.
- After the 12 managed months on the 1-year deal expire, the buyer keeps engine access (just like a lifetime buyer) — they only lose the premium SLA / hands-on support unless they buy another 1-year managed renewal. So the 1-year IS an upgraded managed service that pairs with lifetime access, not a 12-month lease.
- BYOK Gemini Flash Lite = ~$0 LLM cost to us; AIMS GUI hosting is the only marginal cost.

**TOS language (lifetime tier):**

> "Lifetime access" means access for the operational life of the AIMS platform. We reserve the right to modify pricing, support scope, feature coverage, and platform availability at any time. Buyers retain perpetual export rights for their data and configuration. This is not a five-year or absolute-time commitment.

**1-year managed tier SLA:**

> 12 months of explicit support (response SLA: 48hr T1 / 24hr T2 / same-business-day T3). Includes onboarding + managed config. After 12 months, access continues under the standard lifetime terms; managed-service renewal sold separately at then-current rate.
