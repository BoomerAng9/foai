# Owner Approval Boundaries

The system may work autonomously only inside low-risk boundaries.

## Always requires owner approval

- Spending new money.
- Increasing ad budgets.
- Publishing organic, fair-trade, lab-tested, health, or FDA-related claims.
- Changing suppliers.
- Signing contracts.
- Filing legal documents.
- Issuing refunds above the configured limit.
- Publishing final public policy language.
- Making statements about certifications unless verified.

## May be automated after test mode

- Draft captions.
- Classify support messages.
- Create internal summaries.
- Generate first-pass product-copy variants.
- Draft wholesale outreach.
- Prepare daily reports.
- Create research tickets.
- Log receipts.

## Approval receipt

Every approval must include:

- task ID
- requested action
- risk category
- evidence summary
- owner decision
- timestamp
- final output location
