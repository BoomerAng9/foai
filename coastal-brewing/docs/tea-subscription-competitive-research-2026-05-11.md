# Tea Subscription Competitive Research (2026-05-11)

**Purpose:** Calibrate Coastal Brewing Co. tea subscription pricing against the live market. Owner challenged the current $30.99/mo for one 3oz tin as unrealistic. This doc validates that and proposes a defensible re-anchor.

**Method:** WebSearch + WebFetch across 12 tea subscription operators. Price-per-oz and price-per-cup normalized using industry standard 2.5g loose leaf per 8oz cup (so 1oz dry ~= 11 cups; 3oz dry ~= 33 cups).

## Price Bands

**Monthly subscription price band (US tea-of-the-month clubs):**
- **Low:** $10 (Plum Deluxe, Dollar Tea Club entry)
- **Median:** $15-19 (Sips by $15, Atlas $18.95 w/ ship, Smith ~$15-17, BlendBee <$20)
- **High:** $25-30 (Rishi $25, Whistling Kettle $20-30, premium tiers)
- **Outliers above $30/mo:** essentially none for a single-tin curated box. $30+ tiers exist but include multiple oz or accessories (Whistling Kettle Deluxe = 4oz + extras).

**Price-per-oz dry tea band (premium curated):**
- **Low:** $5/oz (Vahdam, BlendBee, Whistling Kettle)
- **Median:** $7-10/oz (Atlas $13.50/oz with shipping is outlier high; pure tea ~$10/oz)
- **High:** $13-15/oz (Atlas Tea Club, Smith sachet equivalent)

**Coastal's current $30.99 / 3oz = $10.33/oz** — sits at the **high end of the median band on $/oz**, but the **monthly box price is ~2× the median** ($15-19). That's the disconnect the owner flagged.

---

## Defensibility Verdict on $30.99/mo for 3oz Tin

**No, $30.99/mo for one 3oz tin is not defensible against the market.** The $/oz ($10.33) is fine — it sits in the premium curated band. The **total monthly outlay is the problem**: market median is $15-19, and $30+ tiers consistently include MORE tea (4oz+) or ADD-ONS (accessories, sachets, second blend).

Customer mental model: "$30/mo for a 3oz tin" reads as "I'm paying $30 and only getting 3oz." They compare against Atlas ($18.95 for 1.4oz worth visiting 2 countries), Sips by ($15 for 4 teas to try), or Plum Deluxe ($10 for 1oz + sample). Coastal loses the doorstep test.

## Recommended Re-Anchor

Hold the **$10-11/oz premium positioning** (justified by Lowcountry provenance, Habbak Saudi mint, ceremonial matcha tier). Reset the **box size and headline price**:

| SKU | Recommended price/mo | Box contents | $/oz | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Tea Monthly (entry)** | **$18.99** | One 2oz tin Lowcountry tea | $9.50 | Lands at market median. 2oz is the meaningful unit for curated premium. ~22 cups/mo = daily-driver. |
| **Tea Bundle (3oz)** | **$24.99** | One 3oz tin (current Coastal SKU re-priced) | $8.33 | If 3oz must stay, this is the right price. Same tin, $6 off current. |
| **Tea + Matcha** | **$34.99** | 2oz Lowcountry tea + 30g ceremonial matcha tin | mixed | Tea band median + matcha premium. Matcha 30g retail ~$25-30 so bundle is favorable. |
| **Tea + Habbak** | **$32.99** | 2oz Lowcountry tea + Habbak Saudi Hassawi mint sachet pack | premium | Habbak rarity justifies $14 premium over Tea Monthly entry. Strong story SKU. |
| **Coastal Chai add-on** | **+$8** | Adds a chai pouch to any tier | n/a | Upsell rail, not standalone tier. |

**Floor check (cost + $1.50 per Coastal canon):** All five tiers clear floor with healthy margin assuming wholesale tea cost $2.50-4/oz inclusive of Habbak.

**Anchor-haggle compatibility:** Catalog MSRP stays at $18.99 / $24.99 / $34.99 / $32.99. Sal/LUC haggle down to $16-22 landing zone for retail Custeez per the negotiation envelope (5-15% Sal auto, 20% conditional). This preserves the haggle theater that doubles as licensee demo reel.

**Annual prepay discount:** ~15% off (industry norm Atlas/Smith/Plum Deluxe all offer). Tea Monthly annual = $193 (vs $228 monthly).
