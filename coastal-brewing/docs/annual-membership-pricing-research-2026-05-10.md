## 3. Pricing Bands That Emerge

- **$0-$75/yr** — Loyalty / breakfast-pass tier (Blue Bottle free, Blackbird Breakfast $75). Pure perks, low commitment.
- **$200-$500/yr** — Curated subscription-box tier (Bokksu $479 prepay). Product + culture/discovery.
- **$700-$1,000/yr** — Premium daily-consumable wellness tier (Magic Mind $711, AG1 $948). Functional product + brand identity + welcome kit.
- **DTC specialty coffee outlier** — Almost **none** of the major DTC coffee roasters (Trade, Atlas, Onyx, Counter Culture, Stumptown, Yes Plz, Blue Bottle, Driftaway) charge a flat annual membership. They monetize per-bag with subscriber discounts. **This is open whitespace.**

## 4. Recommended Coastal Annual-Membership Band

**Recommendation: $149-$249 annual fee** for Coastal Custeez Membership.

Rationale:
1. **Above coffee-loyalty floor ($0-$75).** Coastal is not just a punch card — there's a full agentic experience (Sal_Ang voice, live look-in, hand-pack, member-only haggle floor).
2. **Below wellness daily-consumable ceiling ($700-$948).** Coffee is not a daily medical-grade supplement. AG1/Magic Mind have stronger functional-claim moat.
3. **Aligned to curated-box premium (Bokksu $479)** — but Coastal is not shipping a 20-snack box monthly, so half-to-third of Bokksu's prepay is the right shape.
4. **Whitespace play.** No major DTC coffee roaster has a flat annual membership. Coastal can own the category, especially with the agentic-experience differentiator that no competitor has.
5. **Pooler GA local market.** $149-$249 is comfortable for the Savannah-area specialty/lifestyle buyer (compare local gym memberships ~$50/mo, golf ~$200/mo).

**Suggested tier structure:**
- **Custee Card — $149/yr** — Free shipping, 10% off catalog, member-only haggle (Sal floor 15% pre-loaded), monthly Sal voice check-in, welcome kit with branded mug + first bag
- **Custee Plus — $249/yr** — All of above + quarterly LUC curation drop + reserve allocation for Tier F + ACHEEVY Account Assistant access (1hr/mo) + invite to Pooler tasting events
- **Lifetime Membership — $999 / $4,999** (already in Coastal billing matrix, keep)

**Floor logic:** $149 must cover (a) cost of welcome kit ~$25, (b) average of $40-60 in shipping over a year, (c) ~$15 of agentic-runtime cost. Margin ~$50/yr per member at base tier.

**Anchor → land logic** (per Coastal canon): publish $199/yr as MSRP anchor, allow Sal/LUC to land at $149-$179 during haggle.

**Licensee pitch leverage:** "We're the only specialty coffee brand with a flat annual membership AND an agentic team that earns it." Pitch this band to 7-Brew / Dutch Bros — they currently have NOTHING in this slot.
