This makes Coastal an **Amazon FBM (Fulfilled-by-Merchant) seller with TCR as drop-ship origin**. Amazon's Drop Shipping Policy requires:
1. Coastal is **Seller of Record** — TCR contract must explicitly state Coastal owns inventory at sale
2. Packaging, packing slips, invoices reference **only Coastal Brewing Co.** — never TCR (TCR's wholesale-to-Coastal box brand is invisible to end customer)
3. Coastal handles returns + customer service (route through ACHEEVY/Sal_Ang)

## 3. Amazon Marketplace Economics (TCR-shipped, 12oz Coastal bag)

### Critical fee cliff at $15

Amazon Grocery & Gourmet Food referral fee:
- **8%** on items priced **$15.00 and under**
- **15%** on items priced **above $15.00**

This means $15.99 nets LESS than $14.99. **Never price Amazon SKUs in the $15.00-$16.50 dead zone.**

### Margin table — TCR ships, Coastal pays freight ($6 avg)

Assumed COGS (Coastal's wholesale cost from TCR + Coastal-branded packaging):
- Tier A 12oz: ~$6.50 landed (green ~$3.50, roast/bag/label ~$2, Coastal poly + sticker ~$1)
- Tier F 12oz reserve: ~$9.50 landed

| Listed Price | Referral fee | Ship (Coastal pays) | COGS | NET | Margin |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| $14.99 | 8% ($1.20) | $6.00 | $6.50 | **$1.29** | 8.6% — too thin |
| $15.99 | 15% ($2.40) | $6.00 | $6.50 | **$1.09** | 6.8% — WORSE than $14.99! |
| $17.99 | 15% ($2.70) | $6.00 | $6.50 | **$2.79** | 15.5% — marginal |
| $19.99 | 15% ($3.00) | $6.00 | $6.50 | **$4.49** | 22.5% — OK |
| $24.99 | 15% ($3.75) | $6.00 | $6.50 | **$8.74** | 35.0% — healthy ✓ |
| $32.99 (Tier F) | 15% ($4.95) | $6.00 | $9.50 | **$12.54** | 38.0% — premium ✓ |

### Margin table — TCR ships, BUYER pays $5.99 shipping (recommended)

| Listed Price | Referral fee | COGS | NET | Margin |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| $14.99 | $1.20 | $6.50 | **$7.29** | 48.6% ✓ |
| $19.99 | $3.00 | $6.50 | **$10.49** | 52.5% ✓ |
| $24.99 | $3.75 | $6.50 | **$14.74** | 59.0% ✓ best |
| $32.99 | $4.95 | $9.50 | **$18.54** | 56.2% ✓ |

**Strategy:** Buyer pays shipping at checkout. Match competitor band ($19.99 standard / $24.99 reserve / $32.99 Tier F). Net 48-59% margin on Amazon channel.

### Amazon competitor benchmark (12oz specialty coffee)

- **Stumptown Hair Bender** (12oz): historically ~$15.99 list, S&S as low as $8.39
- **Counter Culture Hologram** (12oz): premium specialty, typically $16-19
- **Onyx Coffee Lab Geometry / Monarch** (12oz): $19-24 premium specialty
- **Death Wish Coffee** (16oz): ~$15-19 mass-market dark
- **Black Rifle Coffee Murdered Out** (12oz): ~$15 mass-market dark
- **Premium specialty 12oz band** (per Coffeeness 2026 review): **$15-$18**

**Coastal positions at $19.99 (Tier A on Amazon) / $24.99 (Tier B-D) / $32.99 (Tier F Reserve)** — clean step above mass-market, in the same band as Onyx, with the agentic-experience differentiator no Amazon competitor has.

## 4. Membership Tier Math (assuming canon $199 Standard Membership baseline)

The existing canon (`coastal-standard-membership-spec-2026-05-10.md`) sets **Standard Membership at $199/yr** with 15% off + free delivery (under $15 freight) + welcome box (V60 + stickers + Habbak tin) + refer-2-fee-returned. **Standard Membership becomes the "Coastal Custee" default tier under the C|Brew umbrella.** Wood Stork and Pooler Pass are NEW additions.

### Coastal Custee (Standard Membership) — KEEP at $199/yr

Already canon, already economically modeled (`+$37 first-year contribution per non-referrer, +$166 per referrer with 2 conversions`). No change recommended. This was correctly anchored to Blackbird Standard Membership's $199 pattern.

### Wood Stork — NEW high-volume / B2B / referrer tier

**Assumed avg behavior:** 24 orders/yr × 8 bags @ $24.99 = **$4,798 GMV/yr** (multi-location B2B, ~2 case orders/mo)

| Price | Hard-$ value | Surplus | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| **$499/yr** ⭐ | $1,402 | $903 | **STRONG — 2.81x value, easy sell** |
| **$749/yr** ⭐ | $1,402 | $653 | **STRONG — 1.87x value, more revenue per Stork** |
| $999/yr | $1,402 | $403 | Premium-Stork; bundle quarterly LP-team site visit |

**Owner-ratified tier prices: $499/yr Standard, $999/yr Reserve.**

**Referral credit mechanic — TIERED PERCENT (owner-ratified 2026-05-10):**

| Cumulative successful referrals | Member's discount on own product orders |
|---|---|
| 0 | 18% (base Wood Stork) |
| 1–5 | 25% |
| 5–10 | 35% |
| 10–20 | 45% |
| 20+ | **50% (max — caps here)** |

Discount caps at 50%. Member doesn't lose anything by referring past 20; the cap just doesn't increase. Discount applies to Wood Stork member's own product orders (NOT membership renewals, NOT wholesale-pricing tiers). Auto-applied via Stripe coupon set `WSTORK_BASE/T1/T2/T3/MAX` per `cbrew-tier-mechanics-spec-2026-05-10.md`.

- **Wood Stork Standard ($499/yr):** 18-50% tiered (per above), free shipping all freight classes (NOT capped at $15), priority Sal_Ang business voice line, 4hr/mo Account Assistant
- **Wood Stork Reserve ($999/yr):** All Standard + dedicated ACHEEVY pod + quarterly LP-team site visit + first-look at licensee pricing tier + named Wood Stork blend + invitation to ACHEEVY founders' table (private quarterly call)

Wood Stork tier is the **direct on-ramp to licensee tier** ($2.5K-$500K/mo from billing matrix Dimension 6) — Reserve members are essentially licensee-prospect incubation.

### Pooler Pass — NEW local tier

**Assumed avg behavior:** 24 in-person visits/yr × 1.5 bags @ $24.99 = **$900 GMV/yr** (twice-monthly local pickup)

| Price | Hard-$ value | Surplus | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| **$49/yr** ⭐ | $204 | $155 | **STRONG — 4.16x value, frictionless POS upsell** |
| **$79/yr** ⭐ | $204 | $125 | Also strong, more revenue per local member |
| $99/yr | $204 | $105 | 2x value cap — still buyable, harder walk-in close |

**Recommendation: Two Pooler Pass tiers — $49/yr Standard, $99/yr Plus.**
- **Pooler Pass Standard ($49/yr):** 12% off in-store, free local pickup (no shipping needed), member-only Pooler events (tasting nights, Sal_Ang storefront kiosk visits), invite to monthly LP-team coffee chat
- **Pooler Pass Plus ($99/yr):** All Standard + monthly Habbak refill (50g tin) + first dibs on storefront-only drops + named "Pooler Local" mention on annual gratitude wall

**Geographic gate (owner-ratified 2026-05-10):** ZIP code enforcement at signup, **50-100 mile radius from 31322**:
- Inside 50 mi → Pooler Pass available (natural local tier)
- 50-100 mi → Pooler Pass available as Extended Local (Beaufort, Statesboro, Brunswick, Hinesville, Charleston-edge)
- Outside 100 mi → system upsells to Coastal Custee Card ($199/yr)

Re-verification at each annual renewal; if member moves out-of-radius mid-year, current year stays valid until renewal date with 30-day conversion notice.
