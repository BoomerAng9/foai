# Feynman Setup for the One Direction System

Feynman is the research and verification lane.

## It is used for

- supplier due diligence
- organic and fair-trade proof
- certification claim checks
- market watch
- competitor research
- product evidence briefs
- public copy evidence review

## It is not used for

- final approvals
- money movement
- Shopify publishing
- supplier changes
- customer support actions

## Typical commands

```bash
feynman "supplier due diligence for Temecula private-label coffee tea fulfillment"
feynman watch "organic coffee and organic tea ecommerce market US"
feynman review ./marketing/product_claims_draft.md
feynman compare "private label coffee tea suppliers for Shopify"
```

## Output rule

Every Feynman output must be saved as a research receipt in:

```text
research/receipts/
```

Then AuditLedger records the receipt path.
