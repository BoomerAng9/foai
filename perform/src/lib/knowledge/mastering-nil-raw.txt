These deals can take many forms, including endorsement, sponsorship, personal appearances, or autograph signings. Student-athletes and their families must understand the terms and conditions of a N.I.L. deal before signing. One example of a N.I.L. deal is a sponsorship agreement with a sports apparel company. In this type of deal, a student-athlete may agree to wear the Company’s clothing and promote their products in exchange for financial compensation. The terms of this Agreement may include the duration of the deal, the amount of payment, and any exclusivity clauses. Another example of a N.I.L. deal is a personal appearance contract.
