## KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR TIE GRADE CALIBRATION

1. **Consensus No. 1**: Fernando Mendoza (Indiana, QB) -- unanimous top-4 across all sources. Heisman winner, National Champion, 3,535 yards / 41 TDs.

2. **Consensus No. 2**: Arvell Reese (Ohio State, EDGE/LB) -- unanimous top-2 across all 3 complete boards. Versatile front-seven defender.

3. **Tier 1 (Top 8)**: Mendoza, Reese, Downs, Love, Styles, Bailey, Bain Jr., Mauigoa -- these 8 appear in every source's top 15.

4. **Biggest variance**:
   - Caleb Banks: Yahoo #9 vs Drafttek #50 (41-spot spread)
   - Blake Miller: Yahoo #8 vs Ringer #46 (38-spot spread)
   - Makai Lemon: Drafttek #11 vs Yahoo #37 (26-spot spread)
   - Dillon Thieneman: Ringer #13 vs Yahoo #39 (26-spot spread)

5. **Ohio State dominance**: 5 Buckeyes in consensus top 35 (Reese, Downs, Styles, Tate, McDonald)

6. **QB class is thin**: Only Mendoza is consensus top-20. Ty Simpson (Alabama) ranges from #20-54. Nussmeier and Allar are mid-round or later.

7. **EDGE-heavy class**: ~10 edge rushers in consensus top 50 (Reese, Bailey, Bain, Faulk, Parker, Mesidor, Howell, Lawrence, Jacas, Thomas)

8. **Positional scarcity**: Only 1 consensus first-round guard (Ioane). Only 1 consensus first-round TE (Sadiq). RB Love is the only RB in top 30 across all boards.
