# Memorandum of Understanding — Reusable Template

A.I.M.S. canonical MOU template, distilled from the IELTS Examiners MOU. Use this as a starting point; reference `examples/mou/ielts-examiners.md` for the example output.

---

# MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

Between **[YOUR COMPANY NAME]** and **[COUNTERPARTY NAME]**

This Memorandum of Understanding ("MOU") is made and entered into on **[DATE]** between **[YOUR COMPANY NAME]**, a [COMPANY TYPE] with its principal place of business at [COMPANY ADDRESS] (hereinafter referred to as "[SHORT NAME]"), and **[COUNTERPARTY NAME]**, an individual / entity with a [mailing address / principal place of business] at [COUNTERPARTY ADDRESS] (hereinafter referred to as the "[COUNTERPARTY ROLE]").

**WHEREAS**, [SHORT NAME] provides [DESCRIBE PRIMARY SERVICES OR BUSINESS]; and

**WHEREAS**, the [COUNTERPARTY ROLE] is qualified to [DESCRIBE QUALIFICATION OR EXPERTISE]; and

**WHEREAS**, [SHORT NAME] desires to engage the [COUNTERPARTY ROLE] to [DESCRIBE PURPOSE], and the [COUNTERPARTY ROLE] desires to provide such services to [SHORT NAME];

**NOW, THEREFORE,** the parties agree as follows:

## 1. Term of Agreement

This MOU shall commence on the date first written above and shall continue until terminated by either party upon **thirty (30) days** prior written notice to the other party.

## 2. Duties of the [COUNTERPARTY ROLE]

The [COUNTERPARTY ROLE] shall perform the following services for [SHORT NAME]:

- [DUTY 1]
- [DUTY 2]
- [DUTY 3]
- [DUTY 4]
- Adhering to [SHORT NAME]'s code of conduct and ethics

## 3. Compensation

[SHORT NAME] shall pay the [COUNTERPARTY ROLE] for the services performed in accordance with the fee schedule established by [SHORT NAME] and communicated to the [COUNTERPARTY ROLE].

## 4. Confidentiality

The [COUNTERPARTY ROLE] shall maintain the confidentiality of [SHORT NAME]'s materials, procedures, standards, and any other confidential information provided by [SHORT NAME] or obtained while providing services under this MOU.

## 5. Intellectual Property

The [COUNTERPARTY ROLE] acknowledges that all intellectual property rights in [SHORT NAME]'s materials, procedures, and standards are owned by [SHORT NAME]. The [COUNTERPARTY ROLE] shall not use or disclose such materials, procedures, or standards except as required in performing services under this MOU.

## 6. Governing Law

This MOU shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of **[GOVERNING JURISDICTION]**.

## 7. Entire Agreement

This MOU constitutes the agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous negotiations, understandings, and agreements, whether written or oral.

**IN WITNESS WHEREOF**, the parties have executed this MOU as of the date first written above.

[YOUR COMPANY NAME]
By: [YOUR NAME], [YOUR TITLE]
Date: ________________

[COUNTERPARTY NAME]
By: [COUNTERPARTY SIGNATURE]
Date: ________________

## Generation guidance for ACHEEVY/Boomer_CFO

When generating an MOU for a user engagement:
1. Read `examples/mou/ielts-examiners.md` as the reference structure
2. Always lead with the WHEREAS clauses establishing context
3. Keep the 7-section structure
4. Match jurisdiction to the customer's region (default GCC if working with KSA partners)
5. The dual-signature block at the bottom is required
