Welcome to A.I.M.S.! We're excited to have you join our team. In the first 90 days, we will closely monitor your progress to ensure you are on the right track for success. The onboarding process is critical to your success and will be crucial to your overall performance.

## Before your first day:
- Review your job description, including responsibilities, expectations, and KPIs
- Confirm the start date and time with HR
- Provide necessary documentation and identification for the HR file

## Week 1:
- Attend orientation and complete required training
- Meet with your manager to discuss goals and expectations
- Set up your workspace and equipment
- Get to know your team and other colleagues

## Week 2-4:
- Attend department meetings and start working on projects
- Identify opportunities for professional development
- Schedule a mid-way check-in with your manager
- Meet with HR to review benefits and any remaining paperwork

## Week 5-8:
- Continue working on projects and achieving KPIs
- Schedule a meeting with HR to discuss performance and identify any challenges or concerns
- Participate in at least one training session or workshop

## Week 9-12:
- Evaluate your progress towards your KPIs
- Meet with your manager for a performance review and identify areas for improvement
- Develop a personal development plan based on your review
- Provide feedback to HR regarding your onboarding experience
