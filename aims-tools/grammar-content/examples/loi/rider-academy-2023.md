ACHIEVEMOR x Rider Academy

Jarrett J. Risher
CEO & Founder
ACHIEVEMOR, Inc.

04/30/23

Jed Duncan
Rider Academy

International Partnership Opportunity: Expanding Rider Safety Training with ACHIEVEMOR

Dear Mr. Jed Duncan,

As the main representative of ACHIEVEMOR Inc., I am writing to express our interest in exploring collaboration with Rider Academy. We have identified two potential options for our partnership, each with its own set of benefits and objectives. Please note that this email contains non-binding provisions of understanding between the parties and does not impose any legal obligations on either party, except as explicitly stated.

**Option 1: Rider Safety Training Collaboration**
In accordance with the Letter of Intent provided by the client, ACHIEVEMOR Inc. will cover all airline, housing, food, medical, and transportation costs and provide a monthly allowance between 4500 to 5500 USD. Rider Academy will represent motorcycle safety instructors willing to come to Saudi Arabia to supervise and/or instruct riding candidates for 90 days.

**Option 2: Acquisition or Strategic Partnership with Rider Academy**
ACHIEVEMOR Inc. is open to exploring the possibility of an acquisition or strategic partnership with Rider Academy, with the goal of establishing a strong presence in the US market and positioning ourselves more aggressively for the pursuit of this project over the long run. The specifics of this option will be discussed in depth with our principal partners and investors, should you prefer to explore this option further.

We understand that time is of the essence and are committed to proceeding with a sense of urgency while maintaining a high level of professionalism. Our goal is to move forward to the next stage of discussions and negotiations while ensuring both parties are comfortable and well-informed.

These two options will be discussed in depth with our principal investors and partners who are the owners of the contract. We kindly request your input and feedback on both options, as well as any additional information that may be relevant for our consideration.

If the terms of this partnership proposal are acceptable to you, please respond to this email or contact us directly to discuss the next steps. We are excited about the potential for this partnership and look forward to working closely with Rider Academy to enhance rider safety training on a global scale.

Thank you for considering this opportunity, and we hope to hear from you soon.

Warm regards,

Jarrett Risher

CEO
ACHIEVEMOR

Signed,
ACHIEVEMOR
By: Jarrett J. Risher
CEO | ACHIEVEMOR

## Why this example matters

This LOI demonstrates the **two-option partnership** pattern — instead of one rigid proposal, ACHIEVEMOR presented Option 1 (the immediate engagement) AND Option 2 (the long-term acquisition/partnership). This:
- Gives the recipient flexibility
- Signals long-term thinking
- Opens the door for higher-leverage outcomes without forcing a yes/no
- References "principal investors and partners" as decision-influencers (creates urgency without ACHIEVEMOR being the bottleneck)

When ACHEEVY/Boomer_CFO generates a partnership LOI, this two-option pattern should be considered for any deal where the upside exceeds simple service provision.
