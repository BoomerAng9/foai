# EXAMPLE: Independent Contractor Agreement — IELTS Examiners

> **Status:** Reference example for the contract-tier escalation that follows a successful MOU. Use after a relationship has been validated via MOU.

> **Source:** Original A.I.M.S. (formerly HIDT) Agreement form.

---

**AGREEMENT FOR IELTS EXAMINERS**

This Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into between A.I.M.S. ("Company") and Daniel Oliver ("Examiner") for the Examiner's provision of services to the Company in connection with the International English Language Testing System (IELTS).

**Services:** The Examiner shall provide IELTS examination services per Company's policies, standards, and procedures mentioned in the MOU.

**Tasks:** The Examiner shall perform duties assigned by Company, including but not limited to conducting speaking and writing tests, evaluating candidate performance, and submitting results to the Company promptly.

**Content:** The Company shall provide the Examiner with appropriate IELTS test materials, including practice materials, to enable the Examiner to fulfill its obligations under this Agreement; unless otherwise agreed, this will be our default course of action.

**Schedule:** The schedule for the provision of services by the Examiner shall be mutually agreed upon by Company and Examiner in writing and shall be subject to change upon the Agreement of the parties.

**Compensation:** The Company shall compensate the Examiner at a rate mutually agreed upon by the parties based on Examiner's experience and qualifications.

**Confidentiality:** The Examiner shall maintain the confidentiality of all examination materials and candidate information by the Company's policies, standards, and procedures and shall not disclose such information to any third party without the Company's prior written consent.

**Ownership:** All examination materials and candidate information provided to the Examiner by the Company shall remain the property of the Company and be returned to the Company promptly upon request or termination of this Agreement.

**Term and Termination:** This Agreement shall commence on the date of execution by both parties and shall continue until terminated by either party upon thirty (30) days written notice to the other party.

**Governing Law and Dispute Resolution:** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed by the laws of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved by arbitration by the Saudi Center for Commercial Arbitration rules.

**Entire Agreement:** This Agreement constitutes the whole Agreement between the parties concerning the subject matter and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous communications and proposals, whether oral or written, between the parties.

IN WITNESS of which, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.

**A.I.M.S. (Print Name and Sign)**

## Why this example matters

This is the **escalation contract** that follows a successful MOU. It moves from a non-binding handshake to a formal binding agreement. Notable features:
- Concise — fits on one page, no fluff
- Lists tasks specifically (not vague service descriptions)
- Confidentiality + ownership are explicit
- Clean termination clause (30 days written notice)
- Arbitration resolution path (Saudi Center for Commercial Arbitration)
- Two-signature block at the bottom

When ACHEEVY/Boomer_CFO needs to escalate a relationship from MOU to binding contract, use this structure as the reference.
