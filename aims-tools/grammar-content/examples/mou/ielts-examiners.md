# MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

Between [Your Company Name] and [Examiner Name]

This Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) is made and entered into on [Date] between [Your Company Name], a [Your Company Type] with its principal place of business at [Your Company Address] (hereinafter referred to as "A.I.M.S."), and [Examiner Name], an individual with a mailing address at [Examiner Address] (hereinafter referred to as the "Examiner").

WHEREAS, A.I.M.S. provides English language testing and training services, including International English Language Testing System (IELTS) examinations; and

WHEREAS, the Examiner is qualified to administer IELTS examinations as an IELTS Examiner; and

WHEREAS A.I.M.S. desires to engage the Examiner as an IELTS Examiner, and the Examiner desires to provide such services to A.I.M.S.;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

**Term of Agreement.** This MOU shall commence on the date first written above and shall continue until terminated by either party upon thirty (30) days prior written notice to the other party.

**Duties of the Examiner.** The Examiner shall perform the following services for A.I.M.S.:

- Administering IELTS examinations in accordance with the policies, procedures, and standards established by A.I.M.S. and IELTS;
- Maintaining the confidentiality and security of IELTS examination materials and results in accordance with the policies and procedures established by A.I.M.S. and IELTS;
- Attending training and standardization sessions as required by A.I.M.S. and IELTS;
- Submitting reports and records related to IELTS examinations as required by A.I.M.S. and IELTS;
- Adhering to A.I.M.S.'s code of conduct and ethics.

**Compensation.** A.I.M.S. shall pay the Examiner for the services performed in accordance with the fee schedule established by A.I.M.S. and communicated to the Examiner.

**Confidentiality.** The Examiner shall maintain the confidentiality of A.I.M.S.'s and IELTS' examination materials, procedures, standards, and any other confidential information provided by A.I.M.S. or obtained while providing services under this MOU.

**Intellectual Property.** The Examiner acknowledges that all intellectual property rights in A.I.M.S.'s and IELTS' examination materials, procedures, and standards are owned by A.I.M.S. and/or IELTS, as applicable. The Examiner shall not use or disclose such materials, procedures, or standards except as required in performing services under this MOU.

**Governing Law.** This MOU shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

**Entire Agreement.** This MOU constitutes the agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous negotiations, understandings, and agreements, whether written or oral.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this MOU as of the date first written above.

[Your Company Name]
By: [Your Name], [Your Title]
Date: [Date]

[Examiner Name]
By: [Examiner Signature]
Date: [Date]
