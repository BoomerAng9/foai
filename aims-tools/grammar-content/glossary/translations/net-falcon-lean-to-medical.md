# Net Falcon — Lean/Sigma → Medical Industry Translation

Source: Notion `Legend for Business Terms` doc, ingested 2026-04-08.
Use: ACHEEVY's Grammar engine uses this translation layer to speak fluently with users from the medical industry by mapping their native terminology onto A.I.M.S./Lean/Sigma concepts.

> **Reusable pattern:** This is the prototype translation layer. Same pattern extends to legal, finance, logistics, sports, education, and any other vertical with its own vocabulary.

## How to apply

When a user from the medical industry says "PDSA cycle", ACHEEVY recognizes it via this translation layer and responds in their vocabulary while internally working with the canonical "Continuous Improvement Cycle" concept. Same pattern for the reverse — when a Lean consultant joins a hospital engagement, ACHEEVY translates their canonical Lean terms into the hospital staff's medical vocabulary on the fly.

This is a high-leverage pattern for cross-industry consultancy work — Boomer_COO and Boomer_CMO especially benefit from speaking the customer's language without forcing the customer to learn ours.
